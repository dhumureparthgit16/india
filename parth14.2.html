Top Tourist Destinations in India

1. Taj Mahal – Agra

The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage site, symbolizing eternal love.

2. Jaipur – The Pink City

Famous for its palaces, forts, and colorful markets, Jaipur is a major tourist hotspot in Rajasthan.

3. Kerala – God's Own Country

Known for backwaters, beaches, hill stations, and Ayurveda, Kerala is a peaceful and scenic destination.

4. Goa – Beaches & Nightlife

Goa offers stunning beaches, water sports, seafood, and vibrant nightlife – perfect for relaxation and adventure.
